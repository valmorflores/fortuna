บ     colorRect(<aRect>, <aColors>) --> self                                   บ
บ                                                                              บ
บ        Directly alters the color of a rectangular group of cells.  <aRect>   บ
บ        is an array of four numbers (top, left, bottom, and right).  The      บ
บ        numbers refer to cells within the data area of the browse display,    บ
บ        not to screen coordinates.  <aColors> is an array of two numbers.     บ
บ        The numbers are used as indexes into the color table for the browse.  บ
บ        These colors will become the normal and highlighted colors for the    บ
บ        cells within the specified rectangle.                                 บ
บ                                                                              บ
บ        Cells that are colored using colorRect retain their color until they  บ
บ        are scrolled off the screen up or down.  Horizontal panning has no    บ
บ        affect on these colors and, in fact, cells that are currently off     บ
บ        screen left or right can be colored even if they are not visible.     บ
บ                                                                              บ
บ        This example colors the entire virtual window (on and off screen):    บ
บ                                                                              บ
บ        aRect := {1, 1, browse:rowCount, browse:colCount}                     บ
บ        browse:colorRect(aRect, {2, 1})                                       บ
